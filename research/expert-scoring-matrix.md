## Scoring Criteria Definitions

| Criterion | What a 10 looks like | What a 1 looks like |
|---|---|---|
| **Real operator** | Currently running campaigns, products, or teams; generating their own results | Commentator only; no demonstrated hands-on execution |
| **B2B SaaS specific** | Every framework and example is native to B2B SaaS | Primary focus is B2C, creator economy, or e-commerce |
| **Content recency** | Published substantive content within days of research collection (June 2026) | No meaningful content in 6+ months |
| **Unique angle** | Covers a topic no other expert in this list addresses | Significant overlap with 2+ other experts |
| **Evidence of results** | Multiple quantified case studies with directly attributable metrics | No documented results; purely theoretical |
