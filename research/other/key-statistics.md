## B2B Buyer Research (Peep Laja / Wynter Data)

| Statistic | Context | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Only 2%** of B2B buyers say their vendors look genuinely different | Survey of 100 marketing leaders; foundational differentiation problem in B2B | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 18, 2026) |
| **98%** of B2B buyers said vendors look the same at least sometimes | Same survey | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 18, 2026) |
| **61%** said vendors look the same often or constantly | Same survey; the majority experience interchangeable vendors | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 18, 2026) |
| **41%** chose a vendor for a genuinely different product capability | Survey of 100 marketing leaders; top single reason for vendor selection | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 17, 2026) |
| **24%** chose "easiest to buy from" | Second-ranked reason for vendor selection | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 17, 2026) |
| **21%** chose "existing relationship or trust" | Third-ranked reason; combined with "easiest to buy from" = 45% of selection decisions | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 17, 2026) |
| **12%** chose price | Fourth-ranked reason for vendor selection | Peep Laja — LinkedIn post (June 17, 2026) |
