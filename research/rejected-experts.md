## Selection Summary

The 10 accepted experts were chosen because they satisfy
the intersection of three criteria simultaneously:

1. Hands-on operator experience (not just commentators)
2. B2B SaaS context (not just general email marketing)
3. Active content output demonstrating current relevance

All 13 rejected experts are credible practitioners.
Rejection reflects scope fit, not quality.
