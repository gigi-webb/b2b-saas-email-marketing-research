## Content

A 4-step guide to build your AI GTM system that never forgets context and actually stays up to date.

Everyone talks about context, but context isn't easily sharable, it gets stale really fast.

The four layers:
1. System of context with foundational .md files so Claude knows your role, industry, company, and work.
2. System of skills for every GTM lane.
3. System of orchestration where agents and hooks decide which skills to run.
4. System of integrations that pull context from or push content to external sources.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

The same four-layer AI GTM architecture applies directly to email operations: foundational context files (ICP, product positioning, past campaign performance) → per-channel skills (subject line generation, lifecycle copy, newsletter drafting) → orchestration hooks that trigger the right skill for the right campaign type → integrations that pull live product and CRM data into every send. B2B SaaS email marketers who build this system once will out-execute teams still briefing Claude from scratch on every campaign.
