## Content

Data from 1,043 companies: 70% of them still target the same customer they started with 3+ years after their first $10k MRR.

Your initial deal size doesn't just shape year one. It gets embedded in your company's DNA.

Most startups begin hunting one of four animals: mice at $10/mo, rabbits at $100/mo, deer at $1,000/mo, elephants at $10,000/mo.

Once you hit $10k MRR, the GTM locks in around that buyer.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

ICP drift is invisible in email programs — lifecycle sequences built for the original buyer persona persist long after the company has moved upmarket or changed target, silently underperforming because the copy, offers, and triggers are calibrated for the wrong customer. B2B SaaS email teams should run an annual audit that compares their current top 20% of customers against the persona their email flows were written for.
