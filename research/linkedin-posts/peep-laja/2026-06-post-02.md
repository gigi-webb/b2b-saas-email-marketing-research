## Content

41% of B2B buyers say they chose a vendor for a genuinely different product capability.

What about the other 59%?

We asked 100 marketing leaders the single biggest reason they picked the winner.

Product capability led at 41%. But right behind it: easiest to buy from 24%, existing relationship or trust 21%, price 12%.

The 59% is a differentiation problem. Or maybe brand opportunity.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

"Easiest to buy from" (24%) and "existing relationship or trust" (21%) together account for 45% of vendor selection — and email is the primary channel for building both. B2B SaaS lifecycle email programs that reduce friction at every conversion step (clear CTAs, frictionless trials, minimal-click upgrades) and that consistently build trust through valuable content are addressing the majority of the buying decision, not just supporting the product's feature differentiation.
