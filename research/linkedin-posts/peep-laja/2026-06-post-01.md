## Content

Only 2% of B2B buyers say their vendors look genuinely different.

Let that land.

We asked 100 marketing leaders how often they think these all basically do the same thing while comparing vendors. 98% said at least sometimes. 61% said often or constantly.

Differentiation isn't a copywriting task you delegate down. It's a risk you authorize from the top.

The blandness in your messaging is the blandness you were willing to approve.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Email is where undifferentiated messaging is most painfully visible — when 61% of buyers think vendors "constantly" look the same, subject lines that sound like every competitor are not just forgettable, they actively confirm the buyer's suspicion that you're interchangeable. The implication for B2B SaaS email programs: differentiation must be encoded in the email brief, not fixed in the subject line rewrite, and that requires leadership to approve a distinct point of view.
