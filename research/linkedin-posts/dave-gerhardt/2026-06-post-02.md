## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

The exclusive private dinner format — specific qualification ($25M+ ARR, in-house operators only), small group (13 people), clear outcome promise ("one or two things that will make you think differently") — is a masterclass in segmented email invitation copy. B2B SaaS teams running event-driven email campaigns should apply the same specificity: who exactly is invited, why them, how many others like them are coming, and what they will concretely leave with.
