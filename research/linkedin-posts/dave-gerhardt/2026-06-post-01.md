## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

The positioning of "real examples, not from Claude" is a deliberate email subject line insight: the most powerful hook for B2B marketers right now is authentic practitioner experience, specifically framed against AI-generated noise. Email programs that surface genuine customer stories, real numbers, and named practitioners — not polished brand copy — will stand out as the inbox fills with AI-generated content that all sounds the same.
