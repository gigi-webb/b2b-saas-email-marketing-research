## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Behavioral personalization at the website-plus-email layer — not just demographic segmentation — is what drove an 850% lift in average customer value in this case. For B2B SaaS, this means the email platform and the website need to share the same behavioral signals: what pages a trial user visited, what features they triggered, what upgrade prompts they saw, so the email they receive next reflects where they actually are in the product journey.
