## Content

It's still early, but it's wild seeing how a few of our power user customers at RightMessage are using our MCP to basically do everything from the comfort of their Claude/ChatGPT chat window.

There's still a lot of educating we need to do around 1) how to best use our MCP and 2) how our MCP can be alongside their other tools (email platform, CRM, analytics tools, ad platforms, etc).

If you know of a SaaS that's doing a great job in educating / pushing their MCP, I'd love to hear about them!

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

MCP integrations are turning email and CRM platforms into tools that can be orchestrated directly from an AI chat window — which means the next generation of email workflows won't be built inside ESPs, they'll be built in Claude. B2B SaaS email teams should evaluate whether their current ESP exposes an MCP server, because that's becoming the interface layer between AI agents and email execution.
