# Don't start on paid until you're killing it on organic — Disagree. This is bad advice.

**Author:** Emily Kramer

## Content

When I posted about paid on LinkedIn a few weeks ago, I got several comments along the lines of "Don't start on paid until you're killing it on organic."

Disagree! This is bad advice.

You should apply learnings from organic to paid and from paid to organic. The best way to do this is to take an audience-first approach to all channels you are running, including paid.

AI makes it so much easier to have an enriched list of accounts and contacts, dynamically feed this into platforms, and take signals from those channels to enrich this list even more. Build this out and all of your learnings compound.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Email, organic content, and paid should share the same enriched audience data — not operate as separate channel silos. For B2B SaaS, this means feeding the same AI-enriched account list into your email platform (for lifecycle and nurture sequences) and your paid platforms simultaneously, so that engagement signals from one channel improve targeting in all others.
