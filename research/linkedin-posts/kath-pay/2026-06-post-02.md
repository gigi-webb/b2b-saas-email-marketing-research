## Content

I remember when women were lucky to get on the email marketing agenda. Keynotes? That was another world entirely.

And some of the conversations that feel almost normal now — psychology, behavioural science, better testing, challenging best practices, questioning the way we have always done things — were once treated as interesting, but not essential.

So when I look around the industry now, I do not just see change. I see progress. I see ideas that were once on the fringe becoming part of the mainstream conversation.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Behavioural science, rigorous AB testing, and evidence-based email strategy have graduated from fringe to mainstream — which means B2B SaaS teams that haven't adopted these frameworks are now behind, not ahead of the curve. The frameworks Kath has been teaching for 27 years (psychology-driven copy, lifecycle segmentation, hypothesis-driven testing) are now the baseline expectation for competitive email programs.
