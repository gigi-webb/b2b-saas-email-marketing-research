## Content

One of the hardest things about traffic going down is managing the executive perception of it.

Organic traffic is down 15% May vs April, but demo requests attributed to organic search are down by 30%. They will assume something is wrong.

But here's what no one considered: The drop in demo requests is a normalization after a few unusually strong months.

It's on YOU, as the channel owner, to surface these insights, control the narrative, and manage the executive perception.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Email is the channel that holds engagement during organic traffic declines — a healthy email list insulates marketing from the executive panic that follows an SEO drop. B2B SaaS email marketers should proactively present email-influenced pipeline data alongside organic traffic metrics so leadership has a complete picture of demand: "organic dropped 15%, but email-nurtured demos increased 8%" is a narrative that protects the program budget.
