## Content

Nearly all of the SEO/GEO problems I am working on today are category misalignment and mismatch problems, not ticky-tacky optimization tricks or GEO hacks.

B2B SaaS brand that's been around for 10+ years has a digital footprint in a legacy category that's now unsexy. Companies setup Profound with a massive list of LLM prompt tracking. The dashboards show zero visibility and they wonder why. The problem is that AI retrieval often follows entity neighborhoods.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Category misalignment is an email deliverability problem too — not technically, but editorially: if your email messaging uses the vocabulary of a legacy category your buyers have moved on from, it fails to resonate even when it lands in the inbox. B2B SaaS email teams with established products should audit whether their subject lines, preview text, and body copy reflect how their current buyers describe the problem, not how the company described the problem when it was founded.
