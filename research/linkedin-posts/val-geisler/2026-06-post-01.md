## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

A blank subject line is one of the strongest curiosity-gap tactics in retention email — it breaks the visual pattern of every other message in an inbox and forces a click out of puzzlement alone. For B2B SaaS re-engagement campaigns targeting ghost or lapsed users, unexpected format can outperform even the best-crafted subject line copy.
