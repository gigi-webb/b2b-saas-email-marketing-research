## Content

The personal brand gurus don't want you to know this but your success in building an audience that actually grows your business is directly correlated to how much proof you share.

There are 8 different types of proof:
Tier 1 Borrowed proof
Tier 2 Self-reported results
Tier 3 Paid media mentions
Tier 4 Solicited testimonials
Tier 5 Earned third-party recognition
Tier 6 Unsolicited customer praise
Tier 7 Demonstrated results
Tier 8 Endorsement from a trusted source

Most experts mostly stick to Tiers 1 and 2. The higher tiers are where clients actually convert.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Email is the highest-leverage channel for deploying higher-tier proof — a single email featuring an unsolicited customer quote (Tier 6) or a demonstrated result with real numbers (Tier 7) converts far better than a case study page that requires the subscriber to go looking for it. B2B SaaS email teams should audit their lifecycle sequences to check how much of their social proof is solicited testimonials (Tier 4) versus the harder-to-earn Tiers 6–8 that actually move buyers.
