# The fastest path to $1M+/year is NOT publishing more content

**Author:** Matt McGarry
**Date:** June 17, 2026

## Content

Creators: The fastest path to $1M+/year is NOT publishing more content, selling digital products, or advertising.

Content is no longer enough to build a creator business. Things changed. Your customers are sophisticated. They don't want another course or info product. AI makes education almost free.

So selling premium content products is harder than ever.

All the creators I know who are doing 7-8 figures now DON'T just create and sell content. Instead, they create products that COMBINE content, community, experience, and implementation into ONE offer.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

Pure content newsletters face the same commoditisation pressure as info products — AI makes information nearly free, so a newsletter that only delivers information is losing its value proposition. B2B SaaS email programs that combine curated insight with community access, live sessions, or implementation support (the Exit 5 model) are building a moat that AI cannot replicate.
