## Content

There's one little-known offer format I'm bullish on in 2026.

AI is quietly killing the traditional content monetization playbook. Customers are choosing LLMs like Claude and ChatGPT INSTEAD of buying your premium content.

The solution? Sell something AI CAN'T deliver. An offer format that combines content, community, experience, and implementation into ONE product.

## Key Insight for B2B SaaS Email

The value of a B2B SaaS email list is no longer measured by information delivered — it's measured by trust built, community accessed, and outcomes enabled. Email programs that exist only to push blog posts and feature announcements are vulnerable; programs that make subscribers feel part of an exclusive peer group doing real work together are not.
